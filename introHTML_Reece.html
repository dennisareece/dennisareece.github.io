Dennis Reece

Biography

Dennis Reece currently lives outside of Nashville, Tennessee. He received his Master’s degree in Curriculum Design and Adult Education from Concordia University. He currently works at UnitedHealthcare and has held several positions since 2013 as a trainer and instructional designer. His current position is with the Community and State division covering Long-Term Services and Supports, as an Instructional Designer. His background knowledge includes over 15 years as an instructional designer and 20 years as a trainer. Mr. Reece began his career with encouragement from friends and family to help start two .com companies in Minneapolis in the 1990’s. During this period, he developed and managed the companies training and consulting divisions. He specializes in applying his background knowledge and expertise in all his training designs and solutions. He has developed: online e-learning modules, virtual instructor lead trainings, and standalone instructional material builds.


Mr. Reece likes to keep his body physically fit by working out daily and taking relaxing cruises. He cares for the community and gives back being involved in numerous charity events. Grateful for an amazing group of friends and family he makes sure people are welcomed in a heartwarming way.
