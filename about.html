Dennis Reece

Hello, my name is Dennis Reece, I am a long-distance learner living in Nashville, Tennessee. Prior to moving to Tennessee, I lived in Phoenix, Arizona for eighteen years. I have a Master’s in Education in Curriculum Design and Learning and Development from Concordia University. I currently work from home at UnitedHealthcare as an instructional designer. I develop online web-based training, assessments, and instructor led courses. My background includes over fifteen years as an instructional designer and twenty years as a trainer. My past work experience has been mainly in the banking and credit industry in which I have held supervisory and executive management positions. I help start two dot.com companies and had my own consulting company for 15 years. My skill set includes: Management, Auditing, Consulting, Instructional Design theories and practices, and background in E-Learning software.
